# Smart Eye Care: User Journey & Booking FAQ

## End-to-End Workflow
1. **Registration**: Patients create a secure account and complete their medical profile.
2. **Lab Visit**: Patients visit a partner lab for a fundus scan. The lab technician uploads images directly to the patient's record.
3. **AI Processing**: The system instantly generates a prediction, confidence score, and Grad-CAM heatmap.
4. **Doctor Assignment**: Calculations route the case to an available ophthalmologist based on specialty.
5. **Medical Review**: Doctors review the AI report, add annotations/notes, and finalize the diagnosis.
6. **Report Delivery**: Patients receive a notification and can view the finalized report in their dashboard.

## Booking FAQ
- **How do I book a lab test?**: Patients can book tests via their dashboard or through the chatbot. They need to select a preferred date and time slot.
- **How do I book a doctor appointment?**: Once a lab report is generated, the system suggests specialists. Patients can book directly from the report view or their dashboard.
- **What documents do I need for a lab test?**: Bring a valid ID. The lab will handle the fundus scan.
- **Can I see my results immediately?**: AI results are generated instantly, but they are typically "Preliminary" until verified by a lab technician or doctor.

## Role-Specific Responsibilities
- **Patients**: Responsible for uploading relevant medical history documents and attending appointments.
- **Lab Technicians**: Responsible for image quality assessment and ensuring clear retinal scans are uploaded.
- **Doctors**: Responsible for final clinical diagnosis, patient consultation, and report annotation.
